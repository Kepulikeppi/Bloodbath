#Possible items in tech tree

#Personal (player character unlocks)

Athlete
- faster moving speed
- faster sprinting speed
- can collect medkits to increase health to 150%
- lucky: when under 10% health, take half damage
- strong hands: +20% melee damage, better recoil control

Barbarian
- can eat two rage pills at a time (for double effect!)
- 50% more melee damage with all melee weapons
- when health is below 20%, gain +100% melee damage

Gunsmith
- can craft bigger magazines for weapons
- can modify weapon barrels (better recoil control)
- can modify loading mechanisms (full auto conversions)
- fast loader: -50% loading time
- can unlock new weapons by using crafting materials
- can upgrade auto-looting drone with a gun

Loot goblin
- enemies drop more loot
- loot gets sucked in from a larger radius
- +100% ammo carrying capacity
- can craft a auto-looting drone
- when picking up a medkit at max health, can convert excess health into XP

Tech guru
- can upgrade auto-looting drone (programmable, faster, etc)
- can hack robot enemies
- can hack terminals to disable automated guard turrets
- can craft a stun gun to stun and hack robot enemies

#Ranged weapons (unlocks and upgrades for guns)

Blok 17 (9mm pistol)
- bigger magazines
  - extended stick magazine, 25 rounds
  - drum magazine, 50 rounds
  - large drum magazine, 100 rounds
- full auto conversion

Jolt Diplomat .44 magnum (revolver)
- longer barrel (more damage, better recoil control)
- speedloader (halves reload time)

Double barrel shotgun
- bore squeeze extension (better damage at range, less spread)
- sawed-off barrel (big spread, diminishing damage at longer range)
